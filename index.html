About Barmos FZE

Established in 2003, Barmos FZE operates at the intersection of Middle Eastern polymer manufacturing and European industrial demand. Our multilingual team and strategic office locations enable direct factory relationships and rapid European delivery.

As an official Taqween distributor for Spain and Europe, we maintain established relationships with leading polymer transformers across GCC countries, enabling preferential pricing and allocation access.
